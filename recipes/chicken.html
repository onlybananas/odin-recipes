Pesto Baked Chicken

Description

This incredibly easy recipe can be cooked by anyone! This four ingredient, weeknight recipe takes 5 minutes to prepare. With the pop of these Italian ingredients, it'll impress anybody.

Ingredients

  • Three chicken breasts
  • Basil-pesto
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella

Instructions

  1. (Optional) Pan sear the chicken until golden. This will give the chicken a nice caramelized taste and it will bake much faster.
  2. Slather a generous layer of basil-pesto on top of the chicken (raw or seared).
  3. Place a couple mozzarella slices on top of the basil pesto.
  4. Finish topping the chicken with thinly sliced tomatoes.
  5. Bake the prepared chicken at 350 degrees until cooked through.
  6. Serve!

Recommended Side Dishes

  • Fries
  • Rice
  • Steamed or baked veggies
  • Baked potatoes
